Resources

Organizing a Brigade? We've got you covered.

Over the past four years, we’ve learned a lot about what works when organizing a Brigade. We’re excited to help you build a sustainable, civic hacking group in your community.

Here are some resources to help you get started:

Organizer’s Playbook

The Playbook gives practical advice and real examples on running a sustainable Brigade.

It includes: guidance on hosting events, building a core team, and practicing user-centered design.

View Playbook

Organizer’s Handbook

The Handbook points to the resources that CfA offers Brigades and how to take advantage of them.

It includes: how to use Expensify, information about the NAC and BATs, and how to get set up on Meetup.

View Handbook

Software Available to Brigades

Learn about the software that vendors have donated for Brigades to use.

Brigade Contact Directory

Want to reach out to a Brigade? Look them up in the up-to-date contact directory.
