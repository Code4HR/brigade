Organizer's Checklist

Code for America has a few requirements for groups that want to join the Brigade network, either as a civic hack night or Official Brigade.

A civic hack night is a regular event that brings government, community partners, and residents together to work quickly and creatively on projects to make their city better for everyone. An Official Brigade is a sustainable, civic hacking group that develops ongoing partnerships with government and community partners to work on projects and host events.

Let us know when you have the requiements in place and we'll help you access the resources Code for America offers.
