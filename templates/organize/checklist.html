Organizer's Checklist

You can join the Brigade Network by organizing a civic hack night or an Official Brigade.

A civic hack night is a good place for new organizers to start. A civic hack night is a regular event that brings government, community partners, and residents together to work quickly and creatively on projects.

An Official Brigade takes a bit more work. An Official Brigade is a sustainable, civic hacking group that develops partnerships with government and community partners to provide strategic support with ongoing projects and events.

Code for America has a few requirements for groups joining the Brigade network, which are listed in the checklist below. If you run into blockers, sign up for Office Hours or join us for a workshop.

Let us know when you have everything in place and we'll help you access Code for America resources.
