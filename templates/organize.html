Want to organize a Brigade? We've got you covered.

Over the past four years, we’ve learned a lot about what works when organizing a Brigade. We’re excited to help you build a sustainable, civic hacking group in your community.

Code for America has a few requirements for groups that want to join the Brigade network, either as a civic hack night or official Brigade. Learn more here.

Once you have all these pieces in place, apply to be a Brigade. You'll then have access to the range of support Code for America offers it's Brigades.
