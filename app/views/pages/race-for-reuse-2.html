From now through December 9th let's campaign together toward a nationwide display of civic engagement at the local level.


Over several recent weeks, civic-minded technologists volunteered in [20] cities across the country to launch civic apps and campaign for civic engagement using those apps.

Each of the apps launched in a participating city is an opportunity for anyone in the city to contribute toward a basic civic function.

In some cities you can use an app to help you "adopt" a fire hydrant, emergency siren, or tree in order to help the city test and maintain those things and communicate with volunteers. In other cities you can take part in creating the online archive of where you live. Help your city and neighborhood document and preserve its history, describe public spaces and map all of that content at the same time!

How it works ↑ Back to top

Campaign support for your city

Local organizers receive a support package to help their city reach achievements for engagement

Code for America broadcasts a national campaign to help people find your city's local engagement activity.

Local organizers and Code for America work together to make sure the national campaign connects effectively with local recruiting

The Brigade Concierge stands at the ready to assist with unmet needs that can be handled remotely.
